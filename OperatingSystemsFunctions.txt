File Management is a function of an operating system.
The operating system keeps track of where things are stored on your computer and the status of EVEY file on your computer!
This function is extremly useful and we often don't even think about it because it is so seamless.

The operating system is in charge of handeling and processing all input and out operations on your computer.
It interfaces with multiple things at once in the correct manner to make the use of input and out as effective as possible.
It also handles the data between different peripheral devices.
